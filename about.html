Want to know About Us ?

myGBUspace is an initiative through which students can collaborate for a project or can hire their fellow mates for the same. Aim is to provide a common platform for seekers and providers. It will also help junior year students to get an insight of how a project is actually build. Along with academics, myGBUspace has an entertainment section which will give updates about on going events in GBU, somewhat like a social media platform only for GBU students.
